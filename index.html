Top Three Activities to do in Baguio ⬇

🛶 Go Ride or Glide at Burnham Park

Take a breezy bike ride, drift along on a swan boat, or relax by the water. This lakeside park is Baguio’s heart—make sure it’s on your list!

🐎 Saddle Up at Wright Park

Channel your inner cowboy and hop on a colorful horse for a short ride under Baguio's pine trees. Perfect for first-timers and a classic local experience you can't skip!

🌸 Join the Bloom at Panagbenga Festival

Catch Baguio in full bloom with dazzling flower floats, street parades, and pure Pinoy pride. It's loud, colorful, and unforgettable—don’t miss the February magic!
